Apple Risotto

Apples...and Rice? Who knew it could be so delicious?

This innovative recipe recreates the taste of real roast pork using a variety of vegtables, spices, bacon, and other umami ingredients! Those who have eaten it have been shocked to learn that there is no pork loin!(The secret is to wrap it all in bacon).

Try it for yourself and see if you can tell the difference!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 apple
  • 1 teaspon of lemon juice
  • 1/4 onion
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1/2 cup of rice
  • 2 tablespoons of white wine
  • 1 cup of apple juice
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 teaspon of granulated consomme
  • 4 slices of thick-cut bacon

Cooking Instructions

  1. Cook the bacon in a frying pan until crisp. Set to the side.
  2. Peel apple and dice it in cubes of 1cm cubed
  3. Mince the onion
  4. Heat the butter in a frying pan and saute the onions
  5. Add the rice to the pan and cook until transparent
  6. Add the white whine to the pan until it's transparent
  7. Mix the apple juice, water and consomm√©. Slowly add this 1/8 cup at a time to (6) simmering on low heat for 15 minutes (until all the liquid is absorbed).
  8. Add the apple dices and lemon juice to (7) and season it to taste with salt and pepper.
  9. Place (8) on a plate and top it with (1).
  10. Serve immediately
